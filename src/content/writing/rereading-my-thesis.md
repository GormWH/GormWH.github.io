Three years on, I re-read the thesis I submitted to UTokyo. The numerical core still holds up — the framing around it I'd rewrite from scratch. Useful exercise: the parts I'd keep are the parts where I committed to a single clear claim, and the parts I'd cut are where I tried to keep every reviewer happy.
